Tasks can be of 2 types:
  scan
  travel

Scan tasks scan for a goal in the nearby area.
Travel tasks make the unit move towards a destination that is far away


Some tasks are relevant only as scan
  fight to defend yourself

Some tasks are relevant only as travel
  build your house

Some tasks are relevant in both cases
  collect resource
     you may know of a particular place with abundunce of iron
     but if you find iron on the way you might as well collect it
